During the second year of the Covid-19 pandemic here in Malta *approximately during May and June of 2021*, me and my colleagues at __AlligentDevs__ were having some fun by trying to guess the amount of positive cases of the succeeding day. My ingenious head thought that just a week before my A'level exams it would be fitting to learn a completely new language, together with a new framework: **Javascript** and **React Native**. This project took approximately a month of my free time, together with AlligentDevs' graphic designer **Justin Byrne** to get to a working prototype (*which looks good*) including back-end and front-end.
