During the second year of the Covid-19 pandemic here in Malta *approximately during May and June of 2021*, me and my colleagues at __Alligent Devs__ were having some fun by trying to guess the amount of positive cases of the succeeding day. My ingenious head thought that just a week before my A'level exams it would be fitting to learn a completely new language, together with a new framework: **Javascript** and **React Native**. 

This project took approximately a month of my free time, together with Alligent Devs' graphic designer [**Justin Byrne**](https://github.com/JMCOfficial), to get to a working prototype (*which looks good*) including back-end and front-end. Special thanks go to back-end developers [**Dzhuliano Dimov**](https://github.com/ALGxMily) and [**Robert Circota**](https://github.com/waterpepene) for inspiration and debugging.
